3 Quotes


“They share a common tendency towards a rebellious creativity aimed at increasing the agency of underdogs.”

When the internet becomes a tool for surveillance form government and  monopolists, In a sense this resistance becomes an act of justice. and an act of pursuing power transparency and equality.

“If the popular imagination hadn’t chained the hacker figure so forcefully to IT, it’s hard to believe it ever would have been demonised in the way it has been, or that it could have been so effectively defanged.”

If one kind of existence is hard to define, then it might harder to subvert or eliminate. The stereotypes from medias really ‘help’.

“ Hacking, in my world, is a route to escaping the shackles of the profit-fetish, not a route to profit.”

The gentrification of hackers are not only about money and property, but also the erosion of subversive elements( like lifestyle etc.), which help people come closer to the reality of our social world. If one wants to revive the original hackers’ spirit, Open-source may be a form, a means, but may not be the whole story.
